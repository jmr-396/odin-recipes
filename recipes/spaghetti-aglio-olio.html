Spaghetti Aglio e Oglio

Description

Spaghetti Agolio e Olio translates simply to "Spaghetti with Garlic and Oil". The beauty of this dish is that it is so simple, yet so tasty! It is a staple dish of Southern Italy, often enjoyed as a midnight snack - it's simple and quick preparation mean you can dish it up in no time!

Ingredients

These ingredients will serve 2-3 people, depending on portion size.

  • 300g Spaghetti
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic (finely chopped)
  • 3 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 Pinch of Chilli Flakes
  • 1 Fist of Flat Leaved Parsley (washed & finely chopped)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add the spaghetti to a saucepan of salted boiling water & cook for 2 minutes less than the minimum time on the packet (Note: You can taste the spaghetti as it cooks to ensure it is salted to your preference)
  2. Add the garlic with the olive oil & chilli flakes to a cold saucepan
  3. Cook the garlic on low heat until it starts to brown
  4. Once the garlic begins to brown, add a ladle of pasta water & increase the heat
  5. Once the pasta is ready, transfer it to the saucepan with the oil (do not discard the pasta water yet)
  6. Add another ladle of pasta water in the saucepan & stir continuously. A creamy sauce will form.
  7. Cook the pasta for another 2 minutes. Add more pasta water as needed - we want any excess water to evaporate by the time the pasta is cooked and al dente
  8. Once cooked, turn off the heat. Add & mix the parsley into the pasta
  9. It's ready. Serve up your delicious Spaghetti Aglio e Olio & Enjoy!
