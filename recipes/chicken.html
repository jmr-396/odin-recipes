Chicken with Mushrooms & White Wine

Description

Tender chicken is simmered in a creamy white wine and mushroom sauce, infused with garlic and herbs. Served over fluffy rice, itâ€™s a comforting and elegant dish perfect for any evening.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast fillets
  • 300 grams white basmati rice
  • 200ml dry white wine
  • 400g closed cap mushrooms (chopped)
  • 1 large red onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 clove of garlic (finely chopped)
  • 2 slices of smoked bacon
  • 1 fist of flat leaf parsley (finely chopped)

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breast with salt & pepper & lightly dust with white flour
  2. Fry the chicken for 14 minutes (7 minutes each side) then remove from the pan and place to the side
  3. Add the onion and garlic to the pan and lightly cook until the onion becomes clear and soft. Add salt.
  4. Add the bacon and cook until brown.
  5. Add the mushrooms to the pan and cover with the lid. Cook until the mushrooms become soft.
  6. Add the chicken back to the pan & add the wine and bring to the boil. Boil for 2 minutes then reduce the heat to low.
  7. Boil the rice in salted, excess water.
  8. While the rice is cooking, add 1-3 ladles of water from the rice to the saucepan. Taste the sauce & add more salt if required.
  9. Once the rice is cooked, drain and leave to the side.
  10. Turn off the heat for the sauce and add the parsley.
  11. Serve with the rice and enjoy!
