Spaghetti Alla Bolognese

Description

Rich and hearty, this classic spaghetti alla Bolognese features a slow-simmered meat sauce made with beef, tomatoes, onions, and Italian herbs. Tossed with al dente spaghetti, itâ€™s a comforting dish full of bold, savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince
  • 500g Spaghetti
  • 2 medium-sized red onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (finely chopped)
  • 500g Passata
  • Mixed herbs
  • Finely grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Begin by frying the onion and garlic on low-medium heat until the onions become clear and soft.
  2. Add the mince and cook until brown.
  3. Add the passata & simmer on low heat. Add a pinch or two of mixed herbs. Taste and add salt.
  4. [OPTIONAL] If you feel the passata you used is too sour, you can use milk/sugar to sweeten it but use sparingly!
  5. Cook the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  6. Drain the pasta, leaving some pasta wasta. Add grated parmesan if you prefer. Mix & enjoy!
